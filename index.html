Our Story & Legacy

Established in 1999, Aquablue Fish Farm started with a vision to empower Indian aquaculture with high-quality, disease-free fish seeds. Under the leadership of Mr. Ashim Sarkar, we have grown from a small local hatchery in Naihati to a national supplier serving over 15,000 farmers across India.


We believe in quality over quantity. Our scientific breeding techniques ensure that every spawn you buy has high survival rates and fast growth potential. Join our family today and grow with confidence!
